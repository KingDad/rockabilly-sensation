[DEMO] Selkie Song

Lately, Brenna has turned her attention away from performance and instead focused on both fiction writing and songwriting. She’s writing a fantasy novel, tentatively called Fallen Among the Folk, which follows a human woman named Mika who is crossing the cultural boundaries of her fantastical world to try to broker peace between the humans like herself and the magical Fae that rule the land. Due to humans in this world remembering all their previous lives, she struggles with the actions of her past. But she is supported throughout the adventure by the gaggle of ridiculous yet charming Fae friends. The story can only be enhanced by the funny, catchy folk tunes that Brenna writes for her characters. She’s also working on a full soundtrack to accompany the reader throughout the story, following the pace of the reader (if they read digitally).
